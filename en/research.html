🧪 Projects

Draft list of recent research threads inferred from Ting's publication and presentation record — let me know if you'd like specific project names, funding sources, or dates added.
  • Pharyngeal Articulation in Amis — Ongoing investigation of pharyngeal consonants and vowel lowering in Amis, with Yueh-chin Chang.
  • Jaw Movement & Prosody (MARRYS Cap) — Collaborative project with Donna Erickson and colleagues developing methods to visualize the role of jaw movement in speech production and prosody.
  • Cantonese Alaryngeal Speech — Acoustic studies of alaryngeal speakers’ intelligibility and clear speech strategies, with Steven Cox, Wei-Rong Chen, and Manwa Ng.

Ting's work combines instrumental phonetic methods (articulatory and acoustic) with corpus-based and experimental approaches to investigate sound patterns in Mandarin, Southern Min, Hokkien, Amis, and other languages of Taiwan and the broader Sinophone and Austronesian world.
