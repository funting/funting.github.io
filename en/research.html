Research

🔍 Specialization

Phonology · Phonetics · The Phonetics–Phonology Interface · The Phonology–Morphology Interface · Chinese Linguistics · Corpus Linguistics · Language Acquisition · Academic Writing

💡 Research Interests

Language contact and variation; tone and prosody; articulatory phonology; coarticulation; Austronesian phonetics

🤝 Affiliations

  • 2019–presentAffiliated Scientist, Haskins Laboratories
  • 2016–2017Postdoctoral Fellow, Department of Linguistics, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

Ting's work combines instrumental phonetic methods (articulatory and acoustic) with corpus-based and experimental approaches to investigate sound patterns in Mandarin, Southern Min, Hokkien, Amis, and other languages of Taiwan and the broader Sinophone and Austronesian world.
