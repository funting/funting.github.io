Lab

Ting's experimental work draws on articulatory, acoustic, and perceptual methods to study speech production and prosody. She has been an Affiliated Scientist at Haskins Laboratories since 2019, and previously conducted phonetics research at the Phonology Circle, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and the Graduate Institute of Linguistics, National Tsing Hua University.
