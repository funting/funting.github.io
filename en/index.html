👋 About

Ting Huang is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Foreign Languages at National Chiayi University, Taiwan. She received her Ph.D. in Linguistics from National Tsing Hua University, where her dissertation on Malaysian Mandarin phonetics and phonology received the Linguistic Society of Taiwan Best Dissertation Award and the Overseas Community Affairs Council Best Dissertation Award. She has held postdoctoral positions at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and National Tsing Hua University, and is currently an Affiliated Scientist at Haskins Laboratories. Her research centers on phonology, phonetics, and the phonetics–phonology interface, with particular interests in tone and prosody, articulatory phonology, coarticulation, and Austronesian phonetics.
